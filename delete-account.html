Delete Your Ariqo Account

Ariqo allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ariqo app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm account deletion.

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by contacting:

Email: mansari2542@gmail.com

Data Deleted

  • Account information
  • User profile data
  • Associated premium account records

Data Retention

Some information may be retained when required by law, security, fraud prevention, or financial record keeping requirements.
